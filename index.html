Protocol Reference

The mechanics of Awakening, in five diagrams.

§ 2

Market Definition

M = ⟨ collateral, loan, oracle, T_M, K, settlement, gates ⟩

Isolated, immutable. Any market may be created permissionlessly. maxDebt = K per unit collateral. Strikes may exceed 100% of spot.

§ 3

Offer-Based Liquidity

Makers publish executable offers off-chain — no locked capital. On take, a callback fires and the maker delivers loan tokens + PAT atomically. Multi-market offers share a consumption budget.

§ 4

Put Attribution Token (PAT)

Every credit unit is backed by a put option at strike K, expiring at T_M. PATs are sourced from three classes: on-chain native (Derive, Premia, Panoptic), off-chain attested (Deribit via Coinbase Custody), or protocol-internal writer pool. Attribution is verified atomically at fill time.

§ 5

Maturity Settlement

At T_M: oracle read once, PATs settle, repayment window opens (24h default). Borrower repays or walks. No interim liquidation path exists in protocol code.

§ 11

Zero-Coupon Collar

Generalized markets attach a call at K_call in addition to the put at K_put. Choosing K_call so C(K_call) = P(K_put) + r_f · τ yields a zero-cost loan — surrendering upside above the cap.
